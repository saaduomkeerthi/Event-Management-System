Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our event management system

How do I create a new event?

After logging in, navigate to the 'Events' section and click on the 'Create New Event' button. You'll be guided through a simple form to add all the necessary details.

Can I manage multiple volunteers for a single event?

Absolutely! Our system allows you to add and assign an unlimited number of volunteers to any event, complete with specific roles and schedules.

Is EMS suitable for large-scale events?

Yes, EMS is built to scale. Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large conference, our robust infrastructure ensures smooth management.
